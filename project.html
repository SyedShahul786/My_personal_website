Project

Door Locking Management System

  1. Microcontroller-Based Control:
    • The system utilizes an 8051 microcontroller, a popular 8-bit microcontroller known for its simplicity and effectiveness. The 8051 is responsible for managing the door locking and unlocking processes, executing control logic, and interfacing with other system components like sensors and actuators.

  2. Authentication and Security:
    • The system can be designed to include various authentication methods such as keypad entry, RFID cards, or biometric sensors. The 8051 microcontroller processes input from these authentication devices to verify user credentials before granting or denying access.

  3. Actuator Integration:
    • The microcontroller controls electronic actuators, such as solenoids or motors, to physically lock or unlock the door based on the authentication results. The system can be programmed to handle different locking mechanisms and provide feedback, such as indicators or alarms, to signal access status..
